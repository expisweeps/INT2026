The Ceremony

INT Traditions

🏆
The Green Jacket

Awarded to the overall net winner. The highest honor in INT golf. Presented at the closing ceremony before the assembled field. Green jacket or death — there is no second place.

⭐
MVP Award

Voted by the field. Recognizes the player who exemplified the spirit, competition, and camaraderie of INT 2026. Not always the best golfer — always the most memorable.

🪙
Infractions

The court of infractions convenes at the closing dinner. All grievances, violations, and memorable moments from the week are heard and judged accordingly. No appeals.

🍷
Flying Horse Ceremony

The closing dinner at Flying Horse Country Club is the capstone of INT. Private room. Full field in attendance. The jacket is awarded. The champion is immortalized.
